About me

Hi！ This is yyxunn

I have always struggled with how to introduce myself, constantly feeling confused by the question, “What kind of person am I?” This uncertainty has also extended to two other profound inquiries: “What kind of person do I aspire to be?” and “What truly constitutes the real world?” It’s possible that I will be entangled in these three questions for the rest of my life. There’s a Chinese proverb, “朝闻道，夕可死矣,” which holds two interpretations. The first suggests that once I have attained a deep understanding of the world, I can peacefully depart from it. The second implies that as I acquire new knowledge, my old self dies, and I embark on a fresh path. I wholeheartedly resonate with both interpretations as they define my personal style.
